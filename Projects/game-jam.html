Siberian Game Jam 2026

Collaborating with a junior team under strict jam constraints taught me the art of prioritization. Post-jam, I took the initiative to fully implement the intended mechanics and atmospheric details that were initially cut due to time.

The Challenge

The project was created during a game jam with limited time and production resources. The main goal was to quickly transform an abstract gameplay concept into a cohesive playable experience with readable mechanics, clear level flow and strong atmosphere.

Design Goals

Readable Gameplay

Ensure that player interactions and navigation remain understandable even during fast iteration.

Atmosphere

Use lighting, composition and environment design to support the emotional tone of the experience.

Player Flow

Build level structure around intuitive movement and visual guidance.

Rapid Production

Create reusable assets and production-friendly workflow under strict deadlines.

My Contribution

Game Design

Created the gameplay concept, designed core mechanics and prepared the initial GDD.

Level Design

Designed the first level layout in Illustrator and later implemented it directly in Unity.

3D Production

Modeled environment assets and interactive prefabs in Blender.

Lighting & Atmosphere

Configured lighting and visual composition to strengthen gameplay readability and atmosphere.
